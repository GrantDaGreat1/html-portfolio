Grant's Top Three Movies

The Mictchells VS The Machines (2021)

The Mitchells VS The Machines is an animated comedy about a family road trip that turns sour thanks to a robot uprising. The movie is funny, heartwarming, and has a visual style equivalent to a comic book. I was debating putting this on here as it has been a few years since I've seen it, but I put it as my favorite movie on another powerpoint, and didn't want to make another horror film, so it earns a spot here.

The Mist (2007)

Directed by Frank Darabont, this movie is based on a Stephen King novella in "The Night Shift." It's a eldritch horror movie that focuses more on us as people and our flaws than the monsters outside. We watched the black & white version in our horror movie class, and it was quickly cemented as one of my favorites.

Iron Lung (2026)

Directed and starred by Mark Fischbach (better known as Markiplier), This movie is a claustrophobic eldritch horror film that is a slow burn and doesn't give much to work with. Although its length could be slightly shorter, it is a well made and creative movie, especially for a self-financed project and Mark's first film.
